Post Malone

Post Malone is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He rose to fame with his 2015 debut single "White Iverson." Post Malone has received six Grammy Award nominations. His 2019 single "Circles" set the record for most weeks in the top ten on the Hot 100.
